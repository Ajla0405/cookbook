Pavlova Cake

braise

90 minutes

8 persons

10/10

Ingredients

    For the Meringue:

  • 4 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon shite vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (cornflour)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    For the topping:

  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar (confectioner's sugar)
  • Fresh berry-fruits sliced as needed
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Making a Pavlova Cake in 6 Steps:

1. Prep and Whip: Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C) and whip 4 egg whites until frothy and stiff.

2.Add Sweetness: Gradually add 1 cup granulated sugar while still whipping until glossy peaks form.

3.Fold and Shape: Gently fold in 1 tsp vinegar/lemon juice, 1 tsp cornstarch, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Spoon onto a baking sheet and shape into a round.

4.Fold and Shape: Gently fold in 1 tsp vinegar/lemon juice, 1 tsp cornstarch, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Spoon onto a baking sheet and shape into a round.

5.Whip Cream: Whip 1 cup heavy cream with 1 tbsp powdered sugar until soft peaks appear.

6Whip Cream: Whip 1 cup heavy cream with 1 tbsp powdered sugar until soft peaks appear..

Remember, pavlovas are delicate, so handle with care when topping and serving.
